Strategic Consortium of
Intelligence Professionals (SCIP)

SCIP is the world’s largest Global Intelligence Association, a non-profit community comprised of over 15,000 individuals in 102 countries, who actively contribute to our mission of promoting, developing, and sharing knowledge and tools around data-driven Competitive, Market, and Human Intelligence in Enterprise, Academia, and Government applications. Our vast pool of accredited educational and training resources, and extensive community engagement and collaboration with Intelligence Thought Leadership, allows us to help achieve positive behaviors and outcomes from driving intelligence as a growth enabler for individuals and organizations alike.

Intelligence for the Betterment of People and Planet

SCIP expands our global leadership in driving Intelligence towards Sustainability and Social Responsibility causes, partnering with organizations around the world to promote data-driven intelligence in tackling major social and economic challenges.

These include the preservation of Earth’s fragile land/water ecosystems, addressing social causes around education, health care, homelessness, and promoting economic and social development by leveraging advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and collaborative innovation in using Intelligence as growth enablers for public and private sector advancement.

SCIP Member Advanced Resources & Training (SMART) Center

The SMART platform is a comprehensive set of Training and Educational curriculum that provides Knowledge and Best Practices towards accredited certification in Competitive Intelligence (CI), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and other processes, tools, and methods within the Intelligence EcoSystem of applications.

STREAM

Similar to traditional podcasts, these are informative videos of individuals or teams showcasing their particular knowledge, processes or best practices in a specific Intelligence area. They vary in length and cover a host of different application areas. These are accessed on-demand from our I_STREAM portal

SCIP Insights

SCIP Insights is our revolutionary world-class Blog platform, providing valuable insights from SCIP and our expanding Intelligence Community of guest Thought Leaders, Instructors, Practitioners and anyone who wants to share their knowledge and learnings on Intelligence EcoSystem applications to the global community.

SCIP Newsletter

SCIP Newsletters are weekly communication share with the greater SCIP community to keep you informed of exciting Intelligence-related content around upcoming Event, Training and Educational opportunities, Member resources, Promotions, Industry News and more!

I_MAG

SCIP’s I_MAG empowers organizations with latest news from the Intelligence EcoSystem, offering use cases, perspectives and ideas from the spectrum of Intelligence Thought-Leadership within the SCIP Consortium Global community

Workshop

SCIP Workshops are learning venues given by some of the top Intelligence Instructors in the world. They cover training and certification of many topics, ranging form Competitive Intelligence tools to Artificial Intelligence (AI) applications, as well as many data and knowledge management modeling exercises. These workshops are guaranteed to provide you the knowledge and expertise to improve your Intelligence skillsets in your organization.

Webinar

SCIP Webinars help you connect with peers, learn best practices and get you the recognition and knowledge from SCIP’s extensive team of world-class instructors and thought leaders in the Intelligence discipline. Our comprehensive portfolio of content covers a wide range of Intelligence application areas along with tools, methods and processes around Competitive Intelligence and the latest AI technology applications

SIC Certification

A formalized set of Curriculum based on 3 Levels of Instruction from Professors at Mercyhurst University – one of the highest regarded Intelligence Training institutions in the world. These 3 levels (of 13 courses in each level) cover everything from the basics of Competitive Intelligence to complex understandings of AI, Human Intelligence, and related intelligence behaviors, understanding, practices and tools, that expand into applications in various Enterprise and Government areas.

AI/Intelligence Certification

This program consists of a series of courses aimed at understanding how AI can impact and improve the intelligence development in various business and social applications by utilizing AI as a means to jumpstart the Competitive Intelligence modeling within your organization.

SCIP Executive Intelligence

This program consists of a series of courses aimed at understanding how AI can impact and improve the intelligence development in various business and social applications by utilizing AI as a means to jumpstart the Competitive Intelligence modeling within your organization.

Recognized for Excellence

SCIP has been awarded the prestigious Accredited Provider designation by IACET (International Accreditors for Continuing Education Training). The ANSI/IACET Standard is recognized internationally as the gold standard for continued education and training. As an Accredited Provider, SCIP offers continuing education credits for its learning events that comply with the Standard.
